Novachat

What started as a project to try out the concepts of broadcasting, turned quickly into a big project.

Novachat is a real-time chatapp build with php laravel.
Someone can make an account, and then you can create a 'channel', in which you can send messages. you can also add other people to your channel.
And as an admin, you can also remove people from the channel, for example.

Do you need an alternative way to send somebody a message? Novachat is here for you!
